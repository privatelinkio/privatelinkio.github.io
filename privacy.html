How Our Extensions Work

All PrivateLink.io extensions follow these core principles:

Local Processing

All processing happens on your device within your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

No Permissions Beyond Necessity

Each extension requests only the minimum permissions required for its specific functionality:

  • Storage Permission: To save user preferences locally (never synchronized)
  • Host Permissions: To operate on specific websites only where needed
  • No Network Permissions: None of our extensions make external network requests

No Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with analytics platforms, tracking services, advertising networks, or external libraries that could compromise your privacy.

Legal Compliance

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

PrivateLink.io is GDPR compliant. Since we collect no personal data, GDPR requirements for data collection, processing, and user rights do not apply. Users retain full control of their local data.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

PrivateLink.io is CCPA compliant. We do not sell, share, or disclose personal information. Users have nothing to opt out of because no data collection occurs.

COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

PrivateLink.io is COPPA compliant. Since we collect no personal information from anyone, there are no special protections needed for children's privacy.

Browser Extension Privacy

Important notes about browser extension privacy:

  • Browser Updates: Chrome regularly updates its privacy and security policies. Visit Google's security page for the latest
  • Your Browser Data: Your browser stores extension preferences. You can manage this in your browser settings
  • No Sync: Our extensions do not sync data across browsers or devices
  • No Cloud: No user data goes to the cloud
