Our Privacy Commitment

Longshot collects ZERO data. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature. This extension operates entirely locally in your browser with no external connections or cloud uploads.

What Data We Collect

None. Longshot does not collect, store, track, or transmit any user data whatsoever. Your screenshots never leave your device unless you choose to share them.

How the Extension Works

Longshot uses intelligent scroll-and-stitch technology to capture full-page screenshots. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Local Capture: Screenshots are captured directly in your browser using Chrome's native screenshot APIs.
  • Local Processing: The scroll-and-stitch algorithm runs entirely on your device. No images are sent to external servers for processing.
  • Local Storage: Captured screenshots are saved directly to your downloads folder. The extension does not store images anywhere else.
  • No Cloud: Zero cloud storage, no image uploads, no external processing services.
  • No Transmission: Zero network requests are made by this extension. Your screenshots remain completely private.

Permissions Explanation

Active Tab Permission

Required to capture screenshots of the current tab when you initiate a capture. The extension only accesses the tab you're actively capturing.

Downloads Permission

Required to save captured screenshots to your downloads folder. Screenshots are saved locally and never uploaded anywhere.

Storage Permission

Used exclusively to save your preferences (such as image format and quality settings) locally on your device. No data is synchronized or transmitted.

Your Screenshots Are Yours

Longshot has no access to your screenshots after they're saved. The files go directly to your downloads folder where you have complete control:

  • You decide where to store them
  • You decide who to share them with
  • You can delete them at any time
  • The extension cannot access saved files

Third-Party Services

Longshot does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or external libraries. The extension is completely self-contained with no external dependencies.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure, breach, or compromise. Your captured content remains entirely on your device and is never touched by our servers (because we don't have any servers).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the extension handles data (spoiler: we won't), we will update this policy and the extension version. However, our commitment to zero data collection is fundamental to Longshot's design and the PrivateLink.io mission.

Privacy by Design

Longshot was built with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought. We can't misuse your data because we never have access to it in the first place. Your screenshots stay on your device, period.
